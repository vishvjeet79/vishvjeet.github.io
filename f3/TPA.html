Thermal Protective Aids

1.made of waterproof materia

2.a thermal conductance of not more than 7,800 W/(m2 K)

3.shall reduce both the convective and evaporative heat loss from the wearer's body

4.cover the whole body of persons of all sizes wearing a lifejacket with the exception of the face

5.Hands shall also be covered unless permanently attached gloves are provided

6.be capable of being unpacked and easily donned without assistance

7.permit the wearer to remove it in the water in not more than 2 min

8.function properly throughout an air temperature range -30°C to +20°C
